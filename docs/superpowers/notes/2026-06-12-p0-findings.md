## 状态根 / 读状态(来自 dump.cs `GameClient` TypeDefIndex 11118）
- `GameClient` 关键字段:
  - `m_GameRoom: ColyseusRoom<GameRoomState>` @0xA8 — 对战房间
  - `m_RoomParser: ProtobufParser` @0xB0 — **解析对战 protobuf**
  - `OnRoomMessageReceived: Action<IMessage>` @0xC8 — **收到对战消息回调 = 读实时态 hook 点**
  - `OnRoomStateChanged: Action<GameRoomState>` @0xD0(GameRoomState 仅大厅态)

- 结论:对战态以 `LC.Google.Protobuf` 的 `IMessage` 经 `m_RoomParser` 解析后由 `OnRoomMessageReceived` 派发。
  - **StateReader 读法(待 Task4 实测确认)= hook `ProtobufParser` 解析方法 或 hook `OnRoomMessageReceived` 派发**,在进程内拿到与代理同样的对战 protobuf,但实时。
